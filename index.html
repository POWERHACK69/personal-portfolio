About me

Hey, I’m POWERHACK—a game developer, consultant, and project manager who isn't shy of crafting weird, thought-provoking, and occasionally unsettling experiences. I blend functional visuals with deep mechanics and narratives. From creating tools with game engines to devouring bugs as a ravenous frog, my game making genres are all over the place!

Need a Godot expert? A narrative designer who can weave immersive stories? A project manager to wrangle your chaotic ideas? Or a game consultant who won’t sugarcoat what’s holding your project back? I’m your guy!
